Character Creation Guide

1. Roll Stats

Begin by rolling for your character's six ability scores: Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma.

2. Choose Race & Class

Select your character's race and class, which will determine their basic abilities, skills, and role in the adventure.

3. Create Backstory

Develop your character's personality, ideals, bonds, and flaws. A rich backstory makes for a more immersive role-playing experience.
